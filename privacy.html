Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Keystone Growth ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit keystonegrowth.org or use our services.

By using our website or submitting your information, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you interact with our website or book a strategy call:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number
  • Business information — company name, service area, current lead flow
  • Usage data — IP address, browser type, device info, referring pages
  • Cookies & tracking — for analytics and ad performance measurement

This information is collected either directly from you or automatically through your interaction with our website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule your strategy call
  • Deliver the services you sign up for
  • Send relevant updates, reports, and follow-up communications
  • Improve our website, services, and marketing
  • Comply with legal obligations

You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only with trusted service providers that help us operate our business. The primary third parties we work with include:

  • Meta (Facebook / Instagram) — for advertising and conversion measurement
  • Google Analytics — for website analytics
  • Calendly — for scheduling strategy calls
  • GoHighLevel — for CRM, email, and pipeline management
  • Formspree — for secure form submissions

These providers are only given access to the information necessary to perform their functions and are bound by their own privacy policies and standard data processing terms.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site and measure advertising performance. This includes:

  • Meta Pixel — used to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to show relevant ads. You can learn more and opt out at Meta's ad preferences.
  • Google Analytics — used to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can opt out via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

You can also control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Lead form submissions (from non-clients) are retained for up to 24 months.
  • Client account data is retained for the duration of our engagement plus up to 7 years for tax, accounting, and legal recordkeeping obligations.
  • Website analytics data is retained according to the settings of the analytics provider (typically up to 26 months).

You may request earlier deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at aaron@keystonegrowth.org.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, including secure systems and restricted access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

9. California Residents (CCPA Rights)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect about you
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

We do not sell or share your personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA.

To exercise any CCPA right, email aaron@keystonegrowth.org with the subject line "CCPA Request." We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business use and are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above reflects the latest revision. Continued use of our website indicates acceptance of any updates.
